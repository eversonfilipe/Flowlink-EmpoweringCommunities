Wireframe Specifications

These high-fidelity wireframes represent the core functionality of the RMSP Water Governance System, designed to address urban water scarcity through integrated governance, real-time monitoring, and multi-level communication. Each interface prioritizes accessibility, simplicity, and user-centered design principles to serve diverse user groups effectively.
